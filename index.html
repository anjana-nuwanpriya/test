ENERGIZED YOUR VIBE WITH "CC"

Cafe Connect is a privately held café based in Kiribathgoda, Western Province, Sri Lanka. It specializes in offering premium coffee, refreshing beverages, and a variety of freshly prepared meals and snacks. The café provides a warm and welcoming environment, ideal for casual meet-ups, professional gatherings, or simply enjoying a relaxing break. Cafe Connect is dedicated to delivering quality flavors, excellent service, and a memorable dining experience for all its guests.

WHERE FLAVOUR MEETS VIBE!

Sri Lanka's culinary journey is one of diversity, heritage, and unforgettable tastes. At Cafe Connect, we celebrate this journey by bringing together the finest flavors from land and sea, served with warmth and creativity. Our promise? To craft not just meals, but memorable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors.

With a passion for quality, a love for community, and partnerships that share our vision, Cafe Connect is more than just a café — it's a place where food, culture, and people truly connect.
