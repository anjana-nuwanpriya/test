About Cafe Connect

"Where Conversations Find Their Home"

Our Story

Our story begins with a simple idea – to create a place where conversations flow as easily as the coffee being poured. At Cafe Connect, the aroma of freshly brewed specialty coffee mingles with laughter, stories, and the spark of new ideas.

Where connections are brewed stronger than the coffee itself.

But coffee is only the beginning. Cafe Connect is a space where flavor, creativity, and celebration come together. From our signature crab dishes that bring the taste of the sea to your plate, to the playful artistry of rolled ice cream crafted before your eyes, every experience here is designed to delight.

We go beyond being a café — offering special rooms for karaoke nights, birthday parties, and private meetings, so your moments with us can be as memorable as they are meaningful. With our BYOB setup, we welcome you to pair your favorite drinks with our dishes, making each gathering uniquely yours.

Just like the great coffee houses of history, Cafe Connect is more than a café. It's a community hub — a cozy corner for dreamers, a meeting point for innovators, and a retreat for anyone who wants to pause, celebrate, and savor life. With every cup, every dish, and every conversation, we're crafting moments that matter.

Founded in 2025

Our Vision

Cafe Connect was built with one vision: to create a modern gathering place in the heart of Kiribathgoda. Much like the timeless coffee houses of old, we are more than just a café – we are a space where people come together to share ideas, spark creativity, and build connections.

Every cup we serve is an invitation to slow down, engage, and connect – with yourself, with others, and with the community around you. Cafe Connect is not just about coffee; it's about conversation, culture, and creating moments worth remembering.

What Makes Us Special

Specialty Coffee

Coffee is more than a drink – it's an experience that grows with every pour. From carefully sourced beans to evolving brewing methods, every sip is an invitation to explore new flavors while honoring the timeless ritual of a shared table.

Signature Crab Dishes

Our culinary passion doesn't stop at coffee. Cafe Connect is home to indulgent crab dishes that bring the flavors of the sea right to your plate. Fresh, flavorful, and crafted with care — it's a dining experience you won't forget.

Rolled Ice Cream

A swirl of delight, crafted before your eyes. Our rolled ice creams are as much a performance as they are a treat — blending creamy textures, playful flavors, and artistry in motion for a dessert that's as fun to watch as it is to savor.

Special Rooms for Every Occasion

Whether it's a birthday party, karaoke night with friends, or a private business meeting, our special rooms are designed to make every gathering memorable. A perfect blend of comfort, privacy, and vibrance — tailored to your needs.

BYOB Setup

We believe great company pairs perfectly with great food. That's why Cafe Connect offers a BYOB setup, giving you the freedom to bring your favorite drinks and enjoy them alongside our signature dishes and experiences.

The Cafe Connect Experience

More than just a café — it's a space to dine, celebrate, create, and connect. Every moment with us is designed to feel special, because it's not just about what's on the table — it's about the memories created around it.
